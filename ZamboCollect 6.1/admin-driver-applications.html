Driver Applications

Review and approve driver applications city-wide. Approved drivers are automatically assigned to their requested barangay.

Admin privilege: Only city administrators can approve or reject driver applications. When approved, the driver is automatically assigned to their requested barangay and gains Driver Portal access. Officers can still suspend drivers through the Complaints feature.
Application Details
Promote to Driver
Are you sure you want to promote this applicant to a Driver account? They will be granted access to the Driver Portal and assigned to their requested barangay.
Once promoted, the applicant's account will be upgraded to Driver role and they will be notified to begin onboarding.
Reject Application
Rejecting this applicant's driver application. Select a reason — they will be notified and may reapply once resolved.
Suspend Driver
You are suspending this driver. Their access will be blocked until you restore it. Please select a reason.
Restore Driver Account
Are you sure you want to restore this driver's account? They will regain full access to the Driver Portal immediately.
The driver will be notified that their suspension has been lifted and their routes restored.
Demote to Citizen
You are about to demote this driver back to a Citizen account. They will immediately lose access to the Driver Portal and all route assignments.
This action cannot be undone from this page. To promote them again, they must submit a new Driver Application.
Reject Profile Change
Rejecting this driver's profile update request. They will be notified and may submit a new request.
