How It Works

From report to resolution
in three steps.

01
Citizen

Report the Problem

A verified citizen spots an illegal dump or overflow bin. They open ZamboCollect, snap a photo, confirm the location, and submit — takes under a minute.

02
Officer

Officer Reviews & Approves

The barangay officer is notified instantly. They review the report, assess priority, and approve it for collection — or flag it for further action.

03
Driver

Driver Collects & Closes

The assigned driver gets the stop added to their daily route. They collect the waste, mark it done in the app, and the citizen is notified of resolution.
