Driver Applications

Review and approve driver applications city-wide. Approved drivers are automatically assigned to their requested barangay.

Admin privilege: Only city administrators can approve or reject driver applications. When approved, the driver is automatically assigned to their requested barangay and gains Driver Portal access. Officers can still suspend drivers through the Complaints feature.
